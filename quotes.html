Quantum Internet

Imagine being millions of miles away and wanting to share huge important piece of information in an hour or two. Woah, that sounds difficult with current technologies. But Quantum Internet is a way of communication to connect distant machines that can transfer information faster than your imagination. Maybe a time comes when it surpasses the speed of light. Despite significant unresolved engineering challenges experts predict a global quantum network will be online by 2030. This is a great news for all people who are interested in quantum technology.In quantum world data is encoded in state of qubits which can be made in quantum computers or processors. A quantum network can send these qubits across various quantum devices which are separated physically. One of advanatges of using quantum internet is the security of data thus providing safer modes of communications all over the globe.
